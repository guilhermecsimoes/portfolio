Front-end

The web development can be divided in front-end and back-end, in a simplified way. The back-end development works with things that are not visible to the users, like the treatment of the data and data sorting, for example. On the other side, the front-end development works with the part of the web that can be viewed by the users, like buttons, icons, layout, and navigation menu. Both developments are very important and can work together, but in some application like the construction of a website the front-end development can work alone.

Geomarketing or Market research

The market research, talking specific of the perception from geomarketing, takes care of the analysis of the geographical environment to make better decisions about the market. In a geomarket analysis it can analyzed consumption data, age, income, population density.

When we think about, for example, the start a gourmet brewery. A lot of specificities of the market can be considered, like: what the price that the consumers are disposed to pay in a differentiated beer? Is there a predisposition to the consumption of gourmet beer? Those people consume more beer at home, at supermarket or pubs?

All these data are analyzed in a market research to conclude which is the best place to open the enterprise.
